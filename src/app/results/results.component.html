Companies are in legacy systems: ERP monolithic or that do not integrate with other existing systems or companies do not have ERP. At level 2, companies are no longer in the legacy of systems. The ERP strategy has been reviewed and updated, or created if none existed. Companies integrate cloud technologies with their ERP and take some of their applications into the cloud. Level 3 is based on the optimization of existing systems by exploiting the possibilities that can bring a gain of productivity, or even a competitive advantage in comparison with the competition. The physical and digital world mix. Companies are mature enough to integrate technologies such as Big Data, Virtual Reality, IoT etc. with their ERP and optimize their use.
